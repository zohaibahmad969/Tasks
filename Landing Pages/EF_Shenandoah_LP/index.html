Professional Preparation for Actual Careers

From its founding, Shenandoah University has always focused on offering professional training to students who are driven by the same goal: graduating with a career track in mind. Whether you major in business, music and performing arts, one of our nationally-recognized pre-health programs, or data science and technology, at Shenandoah you focus on your future career.

200+ areas of study. One university.

Do you have a major in mind already? If so, we probably have it. If not, it’s time for some major exploration! College is a place to investigate all your options. Whether by trying out different classes across the university, or through job shadowing organized by Career Services, we will help you find your passion and purpose.

There are many career paths within each major. Which one will you choose?
